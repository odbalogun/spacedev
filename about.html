Our Story

Building Dreams Into Reality Since 2009

Space Development Consulting Limited (SPACEDEV) was founded with a vision to revolutionize the construction industry in Nigeria through excellence, innovation, and integrity.

What began as a small consulting firm has grown into one of the most trusted names in construction and development across West Africa. Our journey has been marked by landmark projects, satisfied clients, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, SPACEDEV stands as a testament to what dedication and expertise can achieve. From high-rise commercial buildings to sustainable residential communities and critical infrastructure, we have left our mark on Nigeria's evolving landscape.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction and development solutions that exceed client expectations, contribute to economic growth, and create lasting value for communities across Africa.

Our Vision

To be the foremost construction and development consulting firm in Africa, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and transforming communities through world-class infrastructure.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Safety, Sustainability, and Client Focus guide every decision we make and every project we undertake.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our dealings, ensuring transparency and honesty in every interaction with clients, partners, and stakeholders.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from project planning to execution, ensuring that every deliverable meets the highest quality standards.

Safety First

The safety of our workers, clients, and the public is paramount. We maintain rigorous safety protocols and invest continuously in safety training and equipment.

Innovation

We embrace cutting-edge technologies and methodologies, continuously seeking better ways to deliver projects more efficiently and sustainably.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible construction practices, minimizing our ecological footprint while maximizing positive community impact.

Client Focus

Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, understand, and deliver solutions that address their unique needs and exceed their expectations.
